About me

Hi! I am Denis Craciun. I was born in 2002 in Roumania, but I currently live in Italy. I am a college student with a passion for computers. I like coding, cybersecurity and videogames. But how did I end up here? My programming journey began during the first Covid quarentine here in Italy (we are talking about march 2020) when I decided to start learning my first programming language: Python. Since then it became my main programming language, but for sure not my last. I learned my first programming language thanks to FreeCodeCamp and Mark's courses, which explained me the basics of coding and logic. Then I started working on my very first projects, which were mainly easy programs like little games using the Pygame library, algorithms like sudoku solvers and network stuff, like bulding servers and clients. After learning Python I starting to get interested in cybersecurity, and began a new journey which helped me understand how the internet, and a network, works. The person that inspiered me, and helped me a lot during this tangent was John Hammond, with his entertaining hacking tutorials on the TryHackMe website and HackTheBox. After playing a bit on websites like TryHackMe, RootMe, HackerOne and various CTFs, I decided to go back to programming. HackerRank and CodeWars were some other websites where I learned a bit from. I then started to meet with C, as that was the language we were starting at school (and yes, I'm talking about C, and not C++). It was only at this moment that I realised how easy Python really was. Still not happy with what I learned, I decided to learn something about the web development world. That's where FreeCodeCamp helped me again, as it is thanks to them that I learned HTML5 and CSS, which brought me to my first ever web development project: this website. The idea is that I can share the knowledge I gained in this year of programming with everyone, and hopefully help or guide someone through their computer science adventure. I am currently learning better the front-end languages, and I have as my next plan learing React, C# and Java.
